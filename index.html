Art Vandalay

San Francisco Architect Art Vandalay received his Bachelors of Architecture Degree from Columbia University in 1987. Upon graduating he worked for Gensler in Chicago, IL. He is a licensed architect and general contractor who has served hundreds of clients in the San Francisco bay area since 2003.
